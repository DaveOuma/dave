About Me

Engineering the future,
one system at a time.

I am a Software Engineer specializing in scalable backend systems, with a passion for building robust APIs, payment gateways, and educational platforms. I bring a unique blend of technical depth and editorial precision — having reviewed and improved scholarly publications as a Scientific Editor.

As an Educationist, I'm deeply interested in technology-enhanced learning, designing systems that make education accessible, engaging, and data-driven. I work across the stack, from Django and Python backends to React frontends, always with an eye for clean code, security, and user experience.

I am also a Technology Consultant, helping organizations architect digital solutions, integrate payment systems (Pesapal, M-Pesa, Stripe), and adopt cloud-native practices.
